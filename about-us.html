The Team

Who are we?

We are a team of 5 students in Carnegie Mellon University's Master's of Human Computer Interaction Program.

Maggie Bignell

Design Lead

As an accelerated masters student, Maggie is still finishing up her undergrad in Communication Design and HCI. Last summer at Apple she designed interfaces and constructed user flows for the iTunes team.

Lisa Ding

UX Designer
